About Citation Club

Our Mission

To promote high-quality research, proper citation practices, interdisciplinary collaboration, and academic visibility among students and faculty at JIS College of Engineering and beyond.

Our Vision

To build a thriving academic research community that bridges the gap between theoretical knowledge and real-world application across emerging technology domains.

Our Objectives

  • Citation Awareness & Best Practices
  • Research Dissemination & Outreach
  • Knowledge Sharing Across Disciplines
  • Academic Networking & Collaboration
  • Research Impact Tracking & Analysis
